Brute Force Demonstration

This shows how weak keys can be easily guessed (using a very small keyspace for demonstration).

About Brute Force Attacks:

  • In this demo, we're using a 2-byte key (6162 in hex = "ab")
  • Real AES uses 16-byte (128-bit) or 32-byte (256-bit) keys
  • Brute forcing a 128-bit key would take billions of years with current technology
  • This demonstrates why key length matters
